To extremely passionate travelers, Grasp Jalisco is a fictitious trip-planning agency that offers unforgettable authentic Mexican experiences by bringing tourists and locals together while discovering the stunning State of Jalisco.

Our fake team gathers tourists from all over the world, as well as local adventurers, and splits them into diverse groups of eight: six tourists and two locals. Each group then goes on one of our six fantastic tours to:

  • Guadalajara
  • Tlaquepaque
  • Puerto Vallarta New
  • Tequila
  • Chapala
  • Tapalpa
